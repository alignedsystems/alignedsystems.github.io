Dr. Kevin M. Morrin is an independent AI safety researcher and founder of ASRL. His work sits at the intersection of AI governance, digital sovereignty, and expert consensus research. He applies Modified Delphi methodology to examine how policy frameworks develop and how safety controls degrade under institutional and competitive pressures.
